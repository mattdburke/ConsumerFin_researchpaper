    Get your student loan info online
    For students receiving an income from Student Finance England, Wales or Northern Ireland 


    Option 1: Speedy Application (if available)

    1. Choose Speedy Application at the beginning of your Smart-Pig application.
    2. Enter your login details to Student Finance Direct to automatically populate your Smart-Pig application. Please note, we do not save your password or secret answer. 

     Option 2: Manual Application

    1. On the home page, click "Letters and emails" at the top right.
    2. Click on the most recent "Notification of Entitlement" letter.
    3. Click on the PDF and save it to your phone or computer.
    4. When completing your Smart-Pig application, upload your PDF when prompted and make sure your next student finance date is the first day of your next term.

    For students only with SAAS loans
    1. Choose Manual Application at the beginning of your Smart-Pig application.
    2. Scottish Students with a loan through SAAS must photograph or scan the first page of their awards letter to upload during the application.
    For students only with NHS bursaries
    1. Choose Manual Application at the beginning of your Smart-Pig application.
    2. You should have received a letter saying how much you are due to receive on which days.  This letter can be obtained online using the Bursary Online Support System (BOSS)?
    3. Under "Documents" on the BOSS Home page you can download a .pdf of the letter.  Save this to your computer to upload during your Smart-Pig Application.
