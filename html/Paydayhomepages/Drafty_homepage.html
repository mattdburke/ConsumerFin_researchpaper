What’s The Difference?

When it comes to getting cash fast online, it can seem like there are lots of different companies all offering the same thing. However, a Drafty credit line is a new kind of product that can save you a lot of money in the long run. Here’s how we’re different from a payday loan (or any kind of short-term loan) and bank overdrafts.

Payday Loans

A payday loan is a high-interest short-term loan. Usually, you repay them in a month, but some lenders offer payday loans for longer terms. Most people apply for payday loans online, with companies like Wonga, Satsuma or QuickQuid, but there are also some high-street stores that offer them.

There are quite a few differences between a payday loan and Drafty, but here are the two biggest ones:

1
Drafty’s much cheaper than payday loans. Like… MUCH cheaper. Check out more about our charges here (spoiler… no fees!)
2
Once you’ve repaid a loan, that’s it. You have to apply again if you want to borrow again. With Drafty, the cash is available for you to borrow again, up to your approved limit, whenever you want!

So, if you’re looking for a payday loan, then you might want to try Drafty first.
It’s just as fast (and in some cases, faster), it’s cheaper and you can use it again and again.

Overdrafts

An overdraft is connected to your current bank account. It allows you to use more money than is in your account. Depending on how much you use, you’ll probably pay interest on it – how much depends on your bank and whether you’re within agreed limits.

If your overdraft is within your agreed limits, that’s an arranged overdraft. If you go over it, it’s an unarranged overdraft. Usually (though not always), unarranged overdrafts are much more expensive.

Drafty is different from a regular overdraft in a few ways, but these are the two most obvious:

1
Drafty isn’t part of your bank account. Although we can transfer cash to your bank so quickly, you might think we were. Or you might think it’s magic. We’re fine with you thinking that. It’s comforting.
2
Drafty’s cheaper than a lot of overdrafts. And not just cheaper than unarranged overdrafts either. We’re cheaper than some arranged overdrafts, depending how much you want and how long for. We don’t charge any fees either.

Want to find out more? We don’t blame you.
After reading that, we’d want to find out more too.

Ready for the good stuff? Drafty is not an overdraft, a payday loan or a credit card. It is a line of credit, which means you only have to apply once to have access to credit, up to £3,000. Once you’ve opened your line of credit, you can use it up to your approved limit again and again and again and… you get the idea. You can draw any amount of cash up to your limit at any time, and we'll send it to your bank in under 90 seconds*. It’s fee-free too. We don’t want to punish you for using us, so we won’t rip you off with fees. No late fees, no subscription fees, no fees at all.
