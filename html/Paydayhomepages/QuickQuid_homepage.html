How It Works

You Apply

Apply for our flexible QuickQuid payday loans online. The application should only take a few minutes to complete, in which you’ll be asked to provide a few personal and employment details about yourself.

We Decide

After we receive your personal loan application, we'll review your information and provide a loan decision. We'll do our best to make a decision within 30 minutes or less§ so you can move forward with your day.

You Receive

If approved, get short-term loans sent to your bank account within 10 minutes after approval.§ As a new customer, you may receive up to £1000,† and up to £1500† as an existing customer.

Expert Tip

Make sure you shop around and find a lender that offers exactly what you’re looking for. With so many payday lenders to choose from these days, you shouldn't have to settle for less.

Is QuickQuid Right for You?

Short-term loans should only be used for short-term financial needs and are not appropriate for long-term borrowing, or if you are in financial difficulty.
