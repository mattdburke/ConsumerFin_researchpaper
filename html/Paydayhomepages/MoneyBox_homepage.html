Important information

Affordability - Our loans provide a solution for your occasional short-term money needs. They are not suitable for supporting sustained borrowing over longer periods, nor if you are in financial difficulties. The cost of our loans can be higher than other types of loans, such as those requiring you to provide a guarantor, which are typically payable over a longer period, so if you do require a longer term solution there are other more suitable forms of finance available elsewhere. We use the information you provide to help us make a responsible lending decision as to whether you can afford to repay your loan without causing you any financial difficulty. See our questions section below for more information.

Continuous payment authority - Please note that we use continuous payment authorities which are not covered by bank guarantees (unlike direct debits). You are able to cancel a continuous payment authority at any time by contacting us. If you do cancel, you will still owe any outstanding debt and will need to provide an alternative method of repayment on the due date. See our questions section below for more information.

Implications of non-payment - As we report loan information to credit reference agencies, missing payments could adversely affect your credit rating. This may make it more difficult or expensive to obtain credit in the future. We may also pass your account to a debt collection agency or commence legal proceedings to recover the amount you owe. Interest will continue to be added to the amount of credit at the contractual rate on a daily basis until such time as you have repaid what you owe subject to the limits set out in the loan agreement. See our questions section below for more information.

What borrowing risks should I be aware of?

The aim of our payday loans is to provide you with a solution to your occasional short term money needs. They are to solve temporary cash flow problems, not for a long term fix and especially not if you already have money issues. Remember our loans will typically cost you more than other forms of credit such as a car loan or credit card. If you need finance for the longer term, please look elsewhere.

There are serious implications for missing or not making a payment. Consider in the first instance that we report loan information to credit reference agencies. Therefore, missing payments could adversely affect your credit rating. This may make it more difficult or expensive to be approved for finance in the future. Secondly, we may also send your debt to a debt collection agency or commence legal proceedings to recover the balance you owe. Thirdly, your bank may also charge you a fee if you pay us by Direct Debit and that Direct Debit does not clear. You should check if your bank charges for a failed Direct Debit under the terms of your account. Any such charge is unrelated to and not charged by us.

See the question What are the risks of borrowing? on our questions page for more information about what happens if we do not receive payment in full on your repayment day.

If you need to speak to an independent third party about your debt visit www.nationaldebtline.org or www.stepchange.org for advice.

How are you a responsible lender?

247Moneybox is committed to lending responsibly at all times. Our measures include:

  • Carrying out comprehensive credit, ID and employment checks to assess affordability
  • Prohibiting loan extensions
  • Advancing affordable and variable loan amounts based on disposable income
  • Charging interest on a daily basis, as opposed to monthly, so that early repayment can save you money
  • Being transparent, honest and open with you
  • Acting professionally, reasonably and responsibly in all our interactions with you. We will endeavour to make sure that all third parties that we do business with, do the same.
  • Making sure all our staff are highly trained, incorporating all relevant requirements, guidelines and codes set out in law and by the FCA as well as professional trade bodies such as the CFA. This is in addition to our Responsible Lending Principles.
  • Ensuring you have all the information you require to make an informed and sensible borrowing decision. Please contact us at any time if you require extra information.
  • Addressing your questions and handle complaints quickly and efficiently. Our complaints procedure is clearly set out in the Questions and Answers section.
  • Treat cases of financial difficulty in a sympathetic and positive light. For more information please see our Responsible Lending Principles.
