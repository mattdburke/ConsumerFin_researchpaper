12-Month Loans at Pounds to Pocket

If you need money to repair your home, advance your career, or cover the cost of an unexpected surprise, Pounds to Pocket may be able to help! You can apply for one of our loans in a few simple steps.

At Pounds to Pocket, you can complete an online application for one of our 12-month loans, and there is typically no need to spend time filling in or faxing through extra paperwork. If approved, you could even have the funds sent directly to your account within 10 minutes after approval.§


Finance with Straightforward Repayments

At Pounds to Pocket, we understand that how you will repay your loan is one of the most important considerations when you are arranging one of our unsecured loans. Our service is designed such that payments are automatically deducted on the payment due date. Simple monthly payment schedules should help you to budget effectively, and you can rest assured that you will never be penalised for paying one of our short-term loans back early.

To make it easier for you to keep track of your repayments, you will have access to your own personal account where you can view the details of your payment schedule. We offer two options for repayment of our loans, which both enable you to make payments with the minimum of fuss. Payments can be automatically deducted from your debit card on a monthly basis, or alternatively you can make scheduled payments from your bank account.

Whichever option you choose will be straightforward.


Responsible Lender of Unsecured Personal Finance

As well as being committed to offering a first-rate service to our customers arranging unsecured personal finance or unsecured loans, Pounds to Pocket is also dedicated to responsible lending. This means that we always follow responsible lending practices and strive to deliver personal loans to only those customers who qualify.

Additional Information

Maximum and Minimum Repayment Period Duration

Your repayment duration depends upon what works best for you! The minimum period is 6 months and the maximum period is 12 months, but you can always pay off early.

Maximum Annual Percentage Rate (APR)

The maximum APR on a Pounds to Pocket Instalment Loan is 737.1%, but your APR will vary depending on your application details.
