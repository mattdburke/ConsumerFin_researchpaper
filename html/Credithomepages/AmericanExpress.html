Why choose an American Express Credit Card?

 

American Express Credit Cards offer you opportunities to earn a variety of rewards. From leading cashback rates, Avios, SPG points, to loyalty Membership RewardsÂ® that you can redeem for flights, holidays, hotel stays, and gift vouchers.

 

The rewards don't stop there. Many of our Credit Cards offer you Refund Protection, Purchase Protection, online Fraud protection, inclusive insurance benefits, and 24/7 customer support.

 

You can also enjoy access to our exclusive American Express Invites programme, bringing you tickets to some of the hottest shows in town, exclusively for our Cardmembers. Whether your passion is music, film, or theatre, we can get you closer to the action.

 

Key Benefits at a Glance

An American Express travel rewards Card offers you:

 

  • A welcome bonus on selected Cards
  • The ability to earn travel rewards points
  • Purchase protection
  • Online fraud guarantee
  • Travel Accident and Travel inconvenience coverage
  • 24/7 customer service
  • Access to our American Express Invites.

 

24/7 Customer Service

Our dedicated Customer Care Professionals are available 24/7 via phone, text, email or mail. We pride ourselves on customer service and always try to ensure that the first person to answer your call will deal with your needs.

 

Our Global AssistÂ® team is available 24/7 to provide you with help and support when you travel outside the UK.

 

Card Security and Protection

American Express uses secure system and early detection programmes to minimise potential fraudulent activity on your Card.

 

Our Fraud Protection Guarantee means that as long as you are careful with your Card you wont be responsible if you fall victim to fraud.

 

To add security to your purchases, you can register for SafeKey when you shop online, as well as free text and email alerts on your Card activity.

 

There's also the option to add additional protection such as American Express My Essentials and Identity Defence.

Accepted Around the World

When holidaying abroad remember that American Express Cards are accepted globally. Any purchase you make with your Card overseas is converted to pounds sterling as soon as the transaction is processed.

 

International payments are converted to US dollars before being converted into pounds sterling. If your transaction is in US dollars, the amount will be converted directly to pounds sterling.

 

If it's in another currency, it's converted to US dollars and then to pounds sterling.

Manage your Account Online or on Your Mobile

When it comes to managing your Account we offer you easy access to the important information you need. By registering online, you can view and manage your account from any internet enabled device whenever and wherever you choose.

 

You can check your balance, set notifications and reminders, pay your bills and manage your rewards and loyalty points.

Eligibility

 

To save time, use the following checklist to see if you meet our standard eligibility criteria:

 

I am aged 18 or over 

I have no history of bad debt

I have a permanent UK home address 

I have a current UK bank or building society account

Some Cards may have additional specific eligibility criteria, and you will be asked to provide employment, income and bank account information in order to submit your application.

 

Once you have found the Card that best suits you, read the important information, then click the 'Apply now' button on the page to begin the application process. At this point, you can choose optional additional benefits and also decide how you'd like your name to appear on your Card.
