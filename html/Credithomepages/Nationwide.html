    Our cards

    Choose from two great cards, just for members

    Our credit cards are just for members. You're a member when you have a current account, mortgage or savings account with us. Plus, if your main current account is with us, you could get an even better rate with our Select Credit Card. Find out more about membership.

    Want to transfer a balance from another card? Or take a break from paying interest? Whatever your reason for needing a credit card, we've got two great options for you.

    The Select Credit Card

    15.9% APR Representative (variable). Based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200 and a purchase rate of 15.9% p.a. (variable).

    • No annual fee We won’t charge you a monthly or yearly fee for having a credit card with Nationwide

      The Nationwide Credit Card

      Representative example

      17.9% APR Representative (variable). Based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200 and a purchase rate of 17.9% p.a. (variable).

      • Available just for members (who have a current account, mortgage or savings account with us)
      • 0% on balance transfers for 12 months* Balance transfers at 0% must be completed within the first 3 months of account opening
      • No balance transfer fee Applies for the first 3 months of account opening, after which time it will revert to 2.4% (Minimum £5)
      • 0% on purchases for 12 months

        Introductory rate applies from the date the account is opened

      • Commission-free allowance We'll reward you with a commission- free allowance to use abroad on any purchases in pounds sterling
      • Access to exclusive cashback offers When you're shopping online and on the high street, with Simply Rewards
      • No annual fee We won’t charge you a monthly or yearly fee for having a credit card with Nationwide
      • Alerts

        We’ll let you know when you’re close to your credit limit, or a promotional rate on your balance is about to end

      Representative example

      17.9% APR Representative (variable). Based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200 and a purchase rate of 17.9% p.a. (variable).

        • Find out more
        • Apply now

        *Introductory rate applies from the date the balance transfer is applied to the account. Balance transfers at 0% with no fee must be made within 3 months of account opening, each transfer after this time will be subject to a 2.4% (minimum £5) fee, and your standard interest rate. Nationwide reserves the right to withdraw, vary or extend these offers at any time. If you already hold a Nationwide credit card or have closed one in the last 12 months you will not be eligible for the introductory offers.

        Nationwide adheres to The Standards of Lending Practice which are monitored and enforced by the Lending Standards Board (LSB) 

        Credit cards are available to those aged 18 or over (UK residents only), are subject to circumstances and a minimum income of £5,000 per year.
