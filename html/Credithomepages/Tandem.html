READY TO APPLY?

In order to apply for a cashback card, you must:

  • be at least 18 years old
  • currently have an address, and three years address history in the UK
  • have a minimum income before tax of 13,000 per year

When you apply we'll perform a credit assessment. You're more likely to be accepted if you have a good credit history.
