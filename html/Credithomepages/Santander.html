Credit cards

Choose the card that's right for you

We have four cards to choose from. So whether you'd like to save on interest repayments, transfer a balance, spread the cost of purchases, earn cashback, or you’re travelling abroad - we've got it covered.

If you need help choosing the card that's right for you, use our credit card comparison table to see the features of all four cards.

All in One Credit Card

A great range of benefits all on one credit card

For a £3 monthly fee you’ll get:

  • 0% on balance transfers and purchases for 30 months from date of account opening with no balance transfer fee

  • 0.5% cashback on all of your purchases

  • No foreign transaction fees on purchases made abroad in the local currency

Representative example
15.9% p.a. (variable) on card purchases. This is equivalent to 21.7% APR Representative (variable) based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200. Monthly fee: £3.

 

Find out more

Everyday Credit Card

Pay no interest on balance transfers, with no balance transfer fee

  • 0% on balance transfers for 27 months from date of account opening with no balance transfer fee

  • 0% on purchases for 3 months from date of account opening

  • No monthly fee

Representative example
18.9% p.a. (variable) on card purchases. This is equivalent to 18.9% APR Representative (variable) based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200.


 

Find out more

Zero Credit Card

A simple card to give you value at home and abroad

  • No foreign transaction fees on purchases when made in the local currency

  • No cash withdrawal fee anywhere in the world

  • No monthly fee

Representative example
18.9% p.a. (variable) on card purchases. This is equivalent to 18.9% APR representative (variable) based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200.


 

Find out more

Santander World EliteTM Mastercard®

For Santander Select and Private customers

For a £15 monthly fee:

  • Unlimited airport lounge access, global Wi-Fi and concierge service

  • No foreign transaction fees on purchases made abroad when using the local currency

  • Cashback on everyday spend and much more

Find out more about Santander Select and Santander Private

Representative example
18.9% p.a. (variable) on card purchases. This is equivalent to 49.8% APR representative (variable) based on an assumed credit limit of £1,200. Monthly fee: £15

 

Find out more

You can apply for a credit card if you're a permanent UK resident aged 18 years or over, have a gross annual income of £7,500 or more, have a good credit record and have not been declared bankrupt, had a CCJ or an IVA within the last 6 years. We will conduct a credit check as part of the application and this will determine whether or not you're accepted and the credit limit that we can offer. We reserve the right to refuse any application.
