HOW THE CARD WORKS

  • Use your card when you shop
    When you shop in store or online at debenhams.com, and wherever you see the Mastercard sign, use your Debenhams Credit Card.
  • Earn points
    Each time you use your Debenhams Credit Card you’ll automatically earn points:
    You’ll earn 3 points for every £1 you spend at Debenhams and debenhams.com
    And 1 point for every £2 you spend everywhere else
    The more you use your Debenhams Credit Card the more points you’ll earn!
  • Treat yourself with Debenhams Reward Vouchers
    Every three months we’ll convert your points into Debenhams Reward Vouchers and send them to you with your Debenhams Credit Card statement. So you can treat yourself in any UK Debenhams store when you fancy.
  • £5 worth of bonus points in your welcome book
    To help get you off to a flying start, we treat you to 500 Bonus Points* in your welcome book – which gives you a £5 Reward Voucher to spend.

*See our terms and conditions page for further details of these offers

CARD BENEFITS
  • 10% off your first purchase if you qualify for Instant Spend1
  • 500 bonus points worth a £5 voucher in your welcome book2
  • Earn 3 points for every £1 you spend at Debenhams on your Debenhams Credit Card and 1 point for every £2 you spend everywhere else
  • Fee Free Travel Money and preferential rates at the Debenhams Travel Money bureau when you pay with your Debenhams Credit Card3
  • Free delivery on your new purchases – When you make a purchase on debenhams.com  with your Debenhams Credit Card you’ll enjoy free standard delivery.
  • Lunch or afternoon tea for less – Feeling peckish? Treat yourself to something delicious in one of our restaurants or cafés and you’ll save 10% when you pay with your Debenhams Credit Card.
  • More to spend on holiday – Show your Debenhams Credit Card when paying for your travel money at the Debenhams Travel Money bureau and you’ll automatically get a better rate, giving you more to spend on your trip! Plus, if you have a Debenhams Credit Card you can now pay for your Travel Money Fee Free*.

  • Share the love (and reap the rewards) – Earn points more quickly and share the rewards of a Debenhams Credit Card by giving an additional card to your partner or family member. To apply, just call 0333 220 2519.
