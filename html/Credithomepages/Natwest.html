Useful info about our credit cards

How do I apply for a credit card online?

Applying online is easy, safe and secure. It takes about 10 minutes and we ask some basic information about you and your finances. If you bank online with us, we'll also prefill the first stage of your application to make it quicker for you. 

To complete your application you'll need to know:

Your address(es) for the last three years

Your income and employment details

Your bank account details

Details of any cards you wish to transfer a balance from

You can check out this short video guide to find out more about applying online.

Can a credit card help improve my credit score?

You can manage your credit card in a way that could improve your credit score.

When you apply for borrowing, lenders will look to see how you’ve managed your borrowing in the past, and so paying your credit card on time every month and staying within your credit limit can be an effective way to help your credit score.

Where can I use my credit card?

Our credit cards are Mastercard, which means they’re accepted all over the world. They’re also Contactless enabled, which means you can pay for purchases up to £30 at selected UK retailers without needing to enter your PIN.

If paying with your mobile phone is your thing, then we also offer Apple Pay functionality. Simple and easy.

What you should know

Apple Pay is available on selected Apple devices.
