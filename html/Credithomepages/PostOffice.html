Credit Cards

Choose a credit card to suit you - if you need to transfer a balance, spread the cost of larger purchases or a card to use on holiday with no foreign exchange fees.

 

Check if you are eligible for one of our cards before you apply without affecting your credit rating with our Fast Checker service. 

 

Post Office Credit Cards are provided by Bank of Ireland UK. Post Office Limited is a credit broker and not a lender.
