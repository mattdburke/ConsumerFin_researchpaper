The Royal Bank Credit Card

 

The low rate card that saves you money abroad

 

Representative 9.9% APR (variable).

 

To apply, you must be a UK resident, 18+ and earn 10K+ per year.

No foreign transaction fees

No annual fee

Low interest rates

No balance transfer fees

See details and apply

Reward credit card

 

The card that rewards you for shopping

 

Representative 23.7% APR (variable). Annual fee applies. Refunded if you have a Reward current account.

 

To apply, you must be a mainland UK resident, 18+ and earn 10K+ per year.

Earn 1% Rewards in supermarkets (0.5% at their petrol stations)

1% or more at MyRewards retailers

0.5% everywhere else you shop

Annual fee refunded with Reward current account

See details and apply

Reward Black credit card

 

The card that rewards you at home and abroad

 

Representative 37.1% APR (variable). Annual fee applies. Refunded if you have a Reward Black current account.

 

To apply, you must be a mainland UK resident, 18+ and earn 15K+ per year.

No foreign transaction fees

Earn Rewards on your everyday spend

Personalised priority servicing

Annual fee refunded with Reward Black current account

See details and apply

Student credit card

 

The card for undergraduate life

 

Representative 18.9% APR (variable).

 

To apply, you must be a UK resident, 18+ and have a Royal Bank Student current account.

A useful safety net with no annual fee

Maximum credit limit of 500

Build a credit profile

Manage online and in the app

See details and apply
