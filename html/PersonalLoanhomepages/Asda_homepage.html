Make your plans a reality

Whether you are planning on some home improvements, buying a car or simply managing your finances, an Asda Personal Loan could help you make that happen.

You could borrow from £1,000 up to £25,000 and choose to make your monthly repayments over 1 to 7 years.

With one quick and easy application, let Asda search for the best loan rate to suit you

We work with our trusted panel of lenders to ensure we’re offering you the best loan rate for your circumstances. If you’re worried about being accepted, our soft search technology will ensure there’s no affect on your credit score when we check your eligibility.

With only one easy application, you’ll receive an initial lender decision in minutes so you don’t have to put your plans on hold.

And with rates from as low as 2.8% APR variable, a loan from Asda could be more affordable than you think.
