How our loans work

  • You could borrow from £1,000 to £35,000 and choose a repayment period from 1 to 5* years.
  • A quick decision is available online 8am-10pm Monday to Friday, 8am-6pm on Saturday & 9am-6pm on Sunday and during branch and telephone opening hours.
  • You can plan ahead. Have the comfort of knowing what your monthly repayments will be and when your loan will be paid off.
  • Our loans are available to new and existing Clydesdale Bank customers
  • You can repay your loan in full and part at any time and we'll reduce the total charge for credit payable under the agreement. Early repayment charges apply**

Important details

All loans are subject to status. The interest rate offered will vary depending on our assessment of your financial circumstances and your chosen loan amount. The rate offered may differ from the Representative APR shown.

To validate your identity and protect you against fraud, we require details of one of your credit or debit cards (American express or Diners Club cannot be used). A charge of 1p will be made to this account to complete validation and then refunded back to your account. Your card details will not be stored as part of your application or used for any other purposes.

* Loans may be granted over terms of 6 or 7 years if the loan amount is £7,500 or more and, if the purpose of the loan is to repay existing lending with us, the additional amount borrowed must be of greater value than the existing lending that is to be repaid.

** You can repay your loan in full and part at any time and we'll reduce the total charge for credit payable under the agreement. When calculating this reduction we will charge up to an additional 58 days' interest on the amount you repay. You will need to contact our customer services team to inform us of your intention each time you wish to make an additional payment. Our team will be able to provide you with options on how to make this payment.

What happens after you apply

We aim to give online decisions for applications submitted between 8am-10pm Monday to Friday, 8am-6pm on Saturday and 9am-6pm on Sunday. If we are unable to provide a quick decision or your application is submitted outside these times, we will aim to inform you within one working day.

If your loan is accepted online and you choose to print your documents, we'll send you an activation code by First Class post. You will need your activation code to complete the online process.
