Fixed Rate Personal Loan

Now is a good time to make your plans a reality.

Whether you’re planning home improvements, a new car or the wedding of your dreams – you can turn your plans into reality with a personal loan from us, giving you the financial flexibility you need, at a fixed interest rate.

Quick Quote

Loan amount of £10,000 with 60 payments of £196.92 per month. Total amount payable  £11,815.20. Interest rate 6.7% p.a. (fixed). 6.9% APR representative. No arrangement fee applies.

How to apply for a Personal Loan

 

Apply Online or via your First Trust Bank Mobile Banking app
  1. You must be an existing personal current account customer who is registered for Online Services. Sole applicants only.
  2. Apply Online or via your First Trust Bank Mobile Banking app, your application should take approx. 10 minutes.
  3. To find out the decision simply log back into Online or via your First Trust Bank Mobile Banking app.

 

Alternatively you can call us on 0800 38 22 65†.

Applications can take between 15 to 20 minutes to complete.

Or you can select a callback request, complete your details and we will call you back at a time that suits you.

You can also call into your local First Trust Bank branch to complete an application.

We'll be happy to answer any questions you may have about applying for a personal loan with us.

 

† Lines open: 9am to 5pm Monday - Friday (except on bank holidays). Calls may be recorded. Call charges may vary - refer to your service provider.
