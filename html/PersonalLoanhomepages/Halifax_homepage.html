    A LOAN WITH A TWIST

    And now our loans come with a twist! Customers that take out a loan between 24th September and 4th November will be entered into a prize draw, where 15 customers will win their loan back! The prize draw will take place in November.

    Loans will be paid off by the end of December at the latest providing the first repayment has been made.

    3.5% APR Representative on loans between £7,500 and £25,000 over 1 to 5 years.

    Lending and rate are subject to status. This offer is available to UK residents aged 18+ (excluding Northern Ireland)

    Loan with a Twist

    Get started with a Personal Loan from the Halifax

    Whether you're tidying up your finances, making home improvements or need money in an emergency for that broken boiler, our Personal Loan could be the answer.

    You can apply if:

    • you're aged 18 or over
    • you're not in full time education
    • you're a UK resident (excluding the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man)
    • you’re in paid employment or have a regular income.
    • you've not been declined for credit in the last month or have a history of bad credit
    • you’re a new or existing Halifax customer. You are able to apply for a Halifax loan even if you don’t bank with us.

    What you need to know.

    • No charge if you make additional payments
    • If you make an early settlement we may charge you up to 58 days’ interest
    • Make fixed monthly repayments by direct debit until your loan is repaid
    • You can manage your Personal Loan online, in branch or by phone
    • You have the option to apply for up to two repayment holidays a year, subject to approval
    • Repayment holidays are available if you meet certain conditions. Interest will be charged if you take a repayment holiday, so you'll pay more interest overall and your original term will be extended.

    Personal Loan calculator

    Remember that to be eligible for an Personal Loan greater than £25,000, you must have held a Halifax current account for at least 1 month.

    Apply for a Personal Loan

    Applying for our Personal Loan is easy and you could get a decision in minutes.

    • 1 to 7 years available when you apply
    • Fixed monthly repayments
    • No charge for additional payments
    • Quick, easy and secure.

    How to apply

    How to apply

    You can apply below by selecting the correct option based on whether or not you have a Halifax current account.

    I have a Halifax Current Account

    • You can apply for up to £50,000
    • It takes about 1 minute to get your quote in Online Banking
    • Get your quote with no credit searches
    • If you’re approved, you could have the money in your Halifax current account in minutes.

    View an example of our Personal Loan agreement (PDF) for Halifax current account customers.

    Continue to application

    I don’t have a Halifax Current Account

    • You can apply for up to £25,000
    • It takes about 10 minutes to apply online and get a decision
    • If you’re approved, you should have the money in your chosen account within 5 working days.

    View an example of our Personal Loan agreement (PDF) for customers without a Halifax current account.

    Continue to application

    If our Personal Loan isn't right for you consider our other borrowing options.
