Will I qualify for a loan?

At Shawbrook we look at every customer individually - that’s one key reason why we’re different. But we do have some basic criteria which you’ll have to satisfy before we can consider your application. You must

    • Be aged 21 to 75
    • Have been a UK resident for at least three years
    • Have an annual income of at least £15,000
    • Have a personal bank account in the UK

Your loan, your way

You can apply for a Shawbrook Bank Personal Loan for a variety of reasons.

  • You can borrow from £1,000 to £35,000.
  • Loan terms available from 1 to 7 years.
  • You get a fixed rate and fixed monthly payments over the whole term.

Get my personalised quote

What happens next?

Once you fill in our simple online form, you’ll get a personalised rate within seconds. Then, if you decide to go ahead

  1. We’ll ask you to provide a bit more information, such as your bank account details.

  2. At this point you will need to accept the quotation and allow us to complete a full credit search to proceed with the application.

  3. If this is okay, we’ll send you an e-signature pack, with loan documents for you to review and sign.

  4. Once you return your signed documents, the money will be in your account within 3 working days.

  5. Everything’s done online, and there’s no paperwork through the post.
