Loan calculator

Looking for a personal loan but not sure how much your repayments might be? Use our handy loans calculators to find out.

Our unsecured personal loan can be used for many purposes, whether you’re planning some home improvements, getting your finances in order or thinking about buying a new car.

How much we lend and the rate available are subject to our assessment of your circumstances.

Personal Loan calculator

Our Personal Loan calculator will help you gain a better understanding of how much you could borrow and how much your repayments might be. The rate you're offered may differ from the illustrative APR shown – and will be based on your personal circumstances, the loan amount and the repayment term.

Your loan payment schedule is calculated automatically. Please check section monthly repayments to view this information.

Representative example

You could borrow £10,000 over 48 months with 48 monthly repayments of £225.04. Total amount repayable will be £10,801.92. Representative 3.9% APR, annual interest rate (fixed) 3.83%.

This representative APR applies to loans of £7,500 to £25,000 over 1 to 5 years. Other terms and loan amounts are available at different rates. If you qualify for a loan we'll tell you the rate that applies when you get your personalised quote.

APR illustrative

For loan terms more than 5 years log in to get a personalised quotation - it will only take a minute and there's no credit footprint.

Our loans are available to Lloyds Bank current account holders who have had an account for at least one month.

If you are applying online, the funds will be transferred to your bank account immediately between the hours of 9:00am and 8:30pm, or before 9am the following day outside these times.

About the Personal LoanExpandable section

Surprises come in all shapes and sizes. From an unexpected expense to a new opportunity, there are times when you might need quick access to funds. Our Personal Loan could help you get the money you need, when you need it most.

The Personal Loan comes with a variety of options to help meet your needs. You can manage your loan online with Internet Banking, where you can:

  • Make additional payments, pay off your loan early (if you make an early settlement we may charge up to 58 days' interest)
  • Check your transactions and statements
  • Change your details
  • Apply for a repayment holiday (available if you meet certain conditions. Interest will continue to be charged if you take a repayment holiday, so you'll pay more interest overall and your original term will be extended).

Our responsibilities to you

We’ll never encourage you to borrow more than you can afford. To help you get it right, we have lots of help and guidance.

Important information

The factors we consider when setting your Personal Loan rate include:

  • Your credit repayment history
  • Past account history
  • Other personal details held on our records.

View an example of our Personal Loan Agreement (PDF).

Ways to apply for the Personal Loan

To apply for the Personal Loan, you must be over 18 years old and a UK resident. How much we lend and the rate available are subject to our assessment of your circumstances.
